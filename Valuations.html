Transforming The Property Valuation Process

About The Project

The traditional process of estimating the value of a property was extremely slow. Over the years, the financial service channels had been designed in isolation, making the whole experience fragmented and confusing.

My Role: Driven by a wish to adopt a user needs approach and to explore the opportunities within the service, I partnered with Pivotal Labs to kickstart a one of a kind project around the property valuation process. In two years, we built several innovative platforms and tools that disrupted the property lending ecosystem.

The Outcome: We fundamentally changed the way professionals involved in the phases of property valuation go about their jobs. We went from a fragmented and confusing user experience to an easy-to-use, streamlined version.

The Process

User Interviews & In-field Observations

As valuers were trained differently and had diverse ways of approaching the job, I spent time with them in their field to learn about their work in order to create an unified work practice structure versatil enough to serve them all. I followed the study with data synthesis and visualization for easy sharing with the team. I then paired up with subject matter experts to analyze and brainstorm solutions

Solutions & Outcome

A quick, lightweight alternative to full vals

The industry needed a lightweight alternative to expensive reports, so we launched a highly improved version of Desktop Assessment with the ability to do commercial, residential and rural assessment remotely. We incorporated geospatial capabilities and costumed methodology calculators according to the job type.

Supporting Remote Valuations

Because COVID didn't allow valuers to enter the properties, we assisted them by supporting remote valuations. Through a simple step-by-step process, property owners can upload time-stamped images and documentation, along with geo-coordinates, thus reaching assessment compliance.

Geospatial & Machine Learning
