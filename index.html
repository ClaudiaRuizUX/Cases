Simplifying experiences for people

Hello, I am Claudia Ruiz, a designer & researcher that loves data. I had been working for more than 10 years for the media & finance industries.

Optimizing Research for the Construction Industry

Contextual Enquiry  |  Usability Testing  | vMapping & Workshops

Cordell is Australia's largest database for residential, commercial, and mining projects. I engaged with the team to explore the problem and solutions. We improved the data acquisition process providing customers with more value and giving Cordell's team a sense of achievement.

The Challenge

The product was losing customers at a high rate and they were seeking to improve user retention.

The Outcome

An extra 2k new projects per month, serving data 4 times faster. Set the foundations for research to drive future decisions.

Read The Story: About My Role & Process

My Role

I engaged with the team as a solo researcher and designer to explore ways to optimize their tool and processes. This project involved a thorough understanding of the construction domain and user tasks.

  • 01

    Understanding goals: product, user & data

    Stakeholder Interview  |  Analytics & Secondary  | Usability Benchmarking

    Partnered with the team to review the case from their perspective. We highlighted the symptoms of the challenges that were later used to design a contextual observation with users. I observed the usage, problem and general behavior with the system. Later set a benchmark for every stage of the user flow so we could measure the impact of the upcoming changes.

  • 02

    Observing usage & workarounds

    Contextual Enquiry  |  Task Analysis  |  Affinity Mapping  |  Persona 

    Conducted some explorative observation followed by contextual inquiries. Aimed at learning about their process, context, goals with a special focus on usability and workarounds. We used affinity mapping for pattern identification and sense-making and persona for empathy building.

  • 03

    Understanding goals: product, user & data

    Stakeholder Interview  |  Analytics & Secondary  | Usability Benchmarking

    Divided into small teams to built a journey mapping, we visualize all the touchpoints with the legacy system, highlighted user pain points and workaround. Data specialists unpacked to the team the knowledge and challenges to be solved. We captured notes with a mindset of solving problems (HMW) and prioritized around business impact and development efforts.

Teamwork and communication

Collaborative Design Workshops  |  Wireframes & Visual Design  | Prototypes

For users, communication between colleagues was essential

Users needed constant communication with their colleagues so they could synchronize their work. With the old system was common to have duplicated projects and they had to assign ownership verbally. After some idea generation workshops, we had chosen to build the messaging widget, one of the most used pieces of the new system.

Keeping Track of Everything

Collaborative Design Workshops  |  Wireframes & Visual Design  | Prototypes

Users needed a way to organize their work efficiently

To keep track of their workflow, users will surround themselves with paper. To help them we created the recall section: a calendar view with projects sorted by time, a to-do list of jobs.

Capturing Details to Provide Value

Collaborative Design Workshops  |  Wireframes & Visual Design  | Prototypes

Users needed a way to organize their work efficiently

We re-architecture the data with subcategories for the extra division. That allowed them to capture more specific details providing more value to the customer.

Transforming The Valuation Process

Contextual Enquiry  |  User Interviews  | Prototype Testing  | Workshops

CoreLogic is a leading provider of real estate data in Australia and New Zealand. The company was seeking to develop a valuation platform that supports residential, commercial, and rural assessments. Imagine if a valuer could open an app to start assessing the price of a property and the app would be so smart that will already be familiar with the type of construction in that area?

The Challenge

Position CoreLogic as a leader in the valuation industry targeting banking and lenders.

The Outcome

Apps integrated with mapping & machine learning data analysis. Increased converstion rate by 15% & generated investment partnerships 2019-2020.
